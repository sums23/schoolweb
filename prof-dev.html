PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT



Our teachers are intelligent, dedicated and professionals who take their vacation seriously and honour the moral purpose of their work. They are role models for students and are valued by both school and community.

The School considers its dedicated and qualified faculty one of its greatest assets. The faculty is a combination of experienced and young talent.Our teachers keep abreast of latest pedagogical research and incorporate unique and diverse teaching techniques to the classroom.

Faculty regularly discusses and shares books, material, teaching, interdisciplinary workshops and discussions to explore integrated approach to curriculum development.

MES regularly organizes workshops and seminars for management and teaching staff. Topics include teaching methodologies, classroom management and use of information technology in teaching.
